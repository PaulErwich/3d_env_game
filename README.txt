Player controls:

W/S move forward/backward
A/D turn left/right

Shift - sneak

Ship controls:

W/S move forward/backward
A/D turn left/right
Q/E role left/right
CTRL/SHIFT pitch back down, front up

Claw controls:

Either 1 or 2 - up/down
Either 3 or 4 - open/close
Either 5 - toggle claw Picture in picture camera

SPACE - drop boulders you've picked up
